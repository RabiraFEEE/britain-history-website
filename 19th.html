Industrial Revolution

During the 19th century, Britain became the world's first industrial nation. Factories, steam engines, and railways increased production and transportation.

British Empire Growth

Britain controlled many countries in Africa, Asia, and America. It became the largest empire in history.

Queen Victoria Era

Queen Victoria ruled from 1837 to 1901. Her period is called the Victorian Era. Britain experienced economic growth, scientific progress, and cultural change.

Important Inventions

  • Steam engine improvements
  • Railway transportation
  • Telegraph communication
  • Electric power discovery
